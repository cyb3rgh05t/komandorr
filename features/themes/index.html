Themes¶

Customize Komandorr's appearance with beautiful themes.

Available Themes¶

  • Dark - Classic dark theme
  • Plex - Plex-inspired theme
  • Jellyfin - Jellyfin colors
  • Emby - Emby styling
  • And more...

Changing Themes¶

  1. Click your username in top-right
  2. Select Theme
  3. Choose your preferred theme

Themes apply instantly!
